Student Life at Riverbend University

Our Teams

Riverbend University offers a variety of sports for students to join. Teams are named after species native to the Pacific:

  • Seahawks – Soccer
  • Hammerheads – Basketball
  • Orcas – Swimming
  • Sandpipers – Volleyball

Clubs & Organizations

Students can join clubs to explore hobbies, leadership, and community involvement:

  • Environmental Club
  • Robotics Club
  • Debate Society
  • Drama & Theater Club
  • Music & Arts Collective

Frats & Sororities

Greek life at Riverbend University promotes leadership, philanthropy, and campus involvement:

  • Alpha Sigma – Sorority
  • Beta Tau – Fraternity
  • Gamma Phi – Sorority
  • Delta Lambda – Fraternity

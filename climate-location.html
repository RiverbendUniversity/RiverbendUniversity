Climate & Location

Newgrove is a picturesque coastal city in California with a mild Mediterranean climate:

  • Warm, dry summers and mild, wet winters
  • Average annual temperature: 18°C (64°F)
  • Located 5 km from the Pacific coast
  • Surrounded by hills and small rivers providing scenic views

Information & History

  • Riverbend Campus encompasses a wide range of academic areas, including science, literature, languages, and technology. This diversity attracts students from various cultural and social backgrounds. The main buildings, originally government offices built in the twentieth century, now offer a historic environment for study. Its privileged location in town provides students with easy access to all city areas, saving them time and making city resources readily available.
  • In addition to this, the university offers many scholarship options to benefit from, whether your major is related to sports, the arts, or science! Enjoy our top-notch research facilities as well as our sports equipment and fields to develop your academic and physical skills.
  • All of this is possible thanks to our high number of sponsors, who not only help maintain our scholarships and university facilities but also offer various internships at relevant companies that help students economically while they get their degree.

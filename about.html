About Riverbend University

  • Riverbend University, located in the fictional coastal city of Newgrove, California, is a vibrant institution dedicated to academic excellence and student life.
  • Our campus blends modern facilities with beautiful coastal surroundings, providing students with a unique environment for learning and growth.
  • The university offers a wide range of programs across multiple disciplines, from STEM and Engineering to Arts, Humanities, Law, and Literature.
  • Students at Riverbend can join numerous clubs, participate in competitive sports teams, and attend events that foster creativity, leadership, and community involvement.
